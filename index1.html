Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Learn how reducing, reusing, and recycling can help you, your community, and the environment by saving money, energy, and natural resources. Recycling programs are managed at the state and local level find information on recycling in your community.

Reducing and Reusing Basics

The most effective way to reduce waste is to not create it in the first place. Making a new product requires a lot of materials and energy - raw materials must be extracted from the earth, and the product must be fabricated then transported to wherever it will be sold. As a result, reduction and reuse are the most effective ways you can save natural resources, protect the environment and save money.

Our Vision

To inspire and challenge Nigerians, most especially our children, teenagers and youth to achieve the highest waste reduction, recycling and re-use goals in the nation through innovation and creativity, critical thinking and crafts by training them and organizing efficient programs that improves entrepreneural skills and environmental sustainability.

Our mission

At Gold in Dump, we are passionately committed to training young Nigerians on recycling, thus treating our earth and community with respect.

Our Message

Gold In Dump engages in creatively recycling and managing waste products, thereby fully maximizing the potentials in waste. Our experiences in recycling expose children, teenagers, youth and organizations on the hidden treasures in waste thus, reducing the cost of disposing waste. Gold In Dump also encourages 21st century life-long learning skills among our children, teenagers and youth.
